Welcome To Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries

Automated Counselling System

We, the Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM), through this Automated Counselling System, are committed to offering spiritual guidance, encouragement, and support to all in need. Our mission is to provide timely, Bible-based counsel, prayer resources, and direction to individuals facing life’s challenges.
This platform is designed to reach souls across the nations, offering hope, deliverance, and solutions rooted in God’s Word, ensuring that no one walks alone in their journey of faith.

MFM Counselling Ministry

Where Healing Begins, and Deliverance is Made Manifest at Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, we believe that no soul is beyond redemption, no heart too broken to be mended, and no life too entangled to be loosed by the power of the Holy Ghost. Our Counselling Ministry is a sacred space—an altar of restoration—where the weary find rest, the confused receive clarity, and the oppressed encounter deliverance.
Whether you are battling spiritual warfare, navigating emotional trauma, facing marital challenges, or simply seeking divine guidance, our doors are open to you. We are not just counsellors, we are intercessors, spiritual midwives, and vessels of God’s healing power.
What we offer include:

Spiritual Counselling

Life is spiritual. Behind every physical struggle is a spiritual root. Our trained counsellors, grounded in the Word and fire-tested in prayer, will walk with you to uncover hidden battles and apply scriptural solutions through prophetic insight and Holy Ghost-led counsel.

Marriage & Family Counselling

God instituted marriage, and He desires harmony in your home. Whether you're preparing for marriage, struggling with communication, or facing spiritual attacks on your union, we offer biblically sound counsel to restore peace, love, and divine order.

Youth & Identity Counselling

In a world of confusion and compromise, our youth need spiritual anchors. We help young people discover their identity in Christ, navigate peer pressure, and build a life of purpose rooted in holiness and fire.

Why Choose MFM Counselling?

  • Bible-Based Guidance: Every counsel is rooted in the infallible Word of God.
  • Prayer-Driven Solutions: We don’t just talk—we war in the spirit.
  • Confidential & Compassionate: Your story is safe with us.
